Mental Health Research for Justice and Belonging.

Family, Community, and Country

Leads three large projects, including a cohort study with women and children examining intimate partner violence, mental health, functioning, and settlement (NHMRC funded), and intervention with 5 remote First Nations communities focusing on male parenting and adolescent mental health (MRFF funded) and a cohort study of women and children in Timor-Leste examining trauma, cycles of violence, and child development in a conflict-affected setting (NHMRC funded).
